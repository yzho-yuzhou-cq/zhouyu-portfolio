Why Combined wins
Strategy looks calm on paper (σ = 1.22%) but is actually the most violent — 76% of days are flat, and when it moves, it moves hard (kurtosis 73.2, active-day vol 2.46%). Combined blends these bursts with Spot's steady returns, cutting downside deviation to 1.23% (lowest) and pushing Sortino to 1.48 (60% higher than Strategy alone). The result: similar total return, genuinely smooth compounding.
